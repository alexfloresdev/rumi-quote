Things I'm learning

  • Even in scratch projects, define the device you want this on, and hint the answer is always mobile first!
  • You will never escape that question "what device is this for?"
  • Also background pictures with a clear image can distract from the content make your life frustrating as you try to align it just right, so be careful on that
  • A great background image is one that looks good on all devices
  • A bad background image distracts from the content, causes you to obsess over lining things up perfectly and becomes a sink hole of time
  • Skill-Stretch or Skill-Scratch Projects are GREAT so long as your attitude is ...
    1. How can I make this meaningful?
    2. How can I make this the best it can possibly be?
